Government Polytechnic, Ahmednagar established in 1990 and affiliated to MSBTE, had celebrated silver jubilee in 2014. The institute is approved by All India council for Technical Education (AICTE) New Delhi and is affiliated to Maharashtra State Board of Technical Education (MSBTE). The institute has excellent infrastructure, well equipped laboratories, training and placement cell, class rooms, girl's and boy's hostel. The institute is spread over 17.39 acres in the heart of historical city of Ahmednagar which is the only Government polytechnic institute in the district. Ahmednagar is area wise largest district in the state of Maharashtra, India, about 120 km northeast of Pune and 114 km from Aurangabad. The two Holi places Shridi and Shani shignapur is situated in the vicinity of Ahmednagar. The institute is surrounded by two MIDCs namely Nagapur MIDC (12 km) and Supa MIDC (20 km). Ahmednagar is home of Indian Armored Corps Centre & School(ACC&S), Mechanized Infantry Regimental Centre(MIRC) and Vehicle Research and Development Establishment(VRDE). It is with a strong commitment to the quest for excellence in the field of technical education, that Government Polytechnic, Ahmednagar was established in 1990 and is making rapid progress in enhancing its potential and changing its outlook towards providing better environment required to impart the best all round quality technical education laced with morality. We believe that the true potentials of students cannot be realized through academics alone. The students need to be provided with opportunities for exploring and enhancing their talents in extra-curricular activities as well. Accordingly, our academic programs are backed up with appropriate co-curricular and extra-curricular inputs promoting culture, sports, self-governance and values; all of which help the students towards becoming complete human beings. We strive to instil a sense of pride and loyalty in our students. We make every effort to equip our students professionally and personally, intellectually and emotionally.
